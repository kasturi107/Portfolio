Dynamic DCA Backtester

Simulate a risk-weighted Bitcoin dollar-cost-averaging strategy: invest more when BTC risk is low and less (or nothing) when risk is high. Risk is a 0–1 metric approximating Benjamin Cowen's BTC Risk framework, computed automatically from on-chain data (MVRV Z-Score via Coin Metrics). Historical prices come from a bundled historical dataset — no API keys, no manual input.

Price data: Binance daily close (CryptoDataDownload) + CoinGecko for recent 60 days  ·  Risk: MVRV Z-Score (Coin Metrics)
